Our Story

Knooppunt Community Center was founded in 2020 as an initiative of UWC Maastricht to create a space for meaningful community engagement.

The name “Knooppunt” comes from the Dutch word for “junction” or “node,” representing our role as a meeting point where different paths, people, and ideas come together. Our center serves as a hub for intercultural dialogue, learning, and collaboration.
